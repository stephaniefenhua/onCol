Our Mission

Did you know that 56% of women feel unsafe after dark on college campuses?

As female students in and heading into college, our team found itself within these statistics.

Using our coding skills, we strive to build a tool to keep students like us safe. We believe that ALL students should be able to focus on their studies without the constant fear of being harmed. What better solution than a web-app that will help you make friends, too?
